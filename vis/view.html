Global Impact of Sea Level Rise

Brian Lavallee and Daniel Merrell

Overview

Climate change has serious global impacts. This visualization aims to capture some of those impacts as they relate to sea level. The table is populated with coastal countries that will be affected by sea level rise. Select a country and see the impact of sea level rise after 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 meters.


Interactions
  • View each country by clicking on the rows of the table
  • Use the slider to view any country after 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 meters of sea level rise
  • See the data in the table change after moving the slider
  • Sort the columns by clicking on the headers
  • Hover over the bars in the table to see the population density of the impacted region
